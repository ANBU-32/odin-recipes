ChickenBurger

Description

This homemade chicken burger is juicy, flavorful, and incredibly satisfying. Made with tender ground chicken seasoned with herbs and spices, it delivers the perfect balance of taste and texture in every bite.

Served on a toasted bun with crisp lettuce, fresh tomatoes, and your favorite sauces, this chicken burger makes a delicious quick lunch or a hearty dinner. Itâ€™s simple to prepare and far better than any fast-food version!

Ingredients

  • Ground chicken
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Egg
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper
  • Paprika or chili powder
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Mayonnaise or preferred sauce
  • Oil for cooking

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix ground chicken, breadcrumbs, egg, onions, garlic, and spices.
  2. Shape the mixture into equal-sized patties.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and cook the patties until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Toast the burger buns lightly on a pan or grill.
  5. Spread mayonnaise or your favorite sauce on the buns.
  6. Assemble the burger with lettuce, tomato, cheese, and the chicken patty.
  7. Serve immediately while warm.
