Egg Slad

Description

This egg salad is creamy, refreshing, and incredibly easy to make. A mix of boiled eggs, mayonnaise, herbs, and gentle seasoning, it offers a perfect balance of smooth texture and bright flavor.

Whether served in sandwiches, on toast, or as a side dish, this recipe is a simple and satisfying classic. Itâ€™s ideal for quick lunches, meal prep, or a light snack.

Ingredients

  • Boiled eggs
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard (optional)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Spring onions or chives
  • Celery (optional)
  • Lemon juice (optional)
  • Paprika for garnish

Steps

  1. Boil the eggs, cool them, and peel off the shells.
  2. Chop the eggs into small pieces.
  3. In a bowl, combine eggs with mayonnaise, mustard, salt, and pepper.
  4. Add spring onions, celery, or lemon juice if desired.
  5. Mix gently until creamy and well combined.
  6. Serve in sandwiches, on toast, or as a side dish.
