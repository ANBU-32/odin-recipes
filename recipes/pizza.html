Pizza

Description

This homemade pizza features a crisp, golden crust topped with rich tomato sauce, stretchy melted cheese, and your favorite toppings. It’s a comforting and customizable dish that everyone loves.

Perfect for weeknight dinners or fun gatherings, this pizza brings bakery-style quality right to your kitchen. With a few simple ingredients, you can enjoy a fresh, flavorful slice anytime.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Vegetables (bell peppers, onions, tomatoes)
  • Pepperoni or preferred toppings
  • Oregano and chili flakes
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to a high temperature (220–250°C).
  2. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your preferred thickness.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add a generous layer of mozzarella cheese.
  5. Place your favorite toppings evenly over the pizza.
  6. Drizzle a little olive oil on top.
  7. Bake for 10–15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  8. Sprinkle oregano and chili flakes before serving.
