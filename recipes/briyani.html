Briyani

Description

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish layered with fragrant spices, tender meat, and basmati rice. It is a classic South Asian recipe known for its rich taste and festive appeal.

This recipe brings together saffron-infused rice, perfectly seasoned meat, and slow cooking to create a delicious, balanced, and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken or vegetables
  • Onions, sliced
  • Tomatoes, chopped
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Yogurt
  • Biryani masala
  • Turmeric and red chili powder
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon)
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Saffron or food coloring (optional)
  • Oil or ghee
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 20–30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pot and sauté sliced onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and spices, then cook until fragrant.
  4. Add chicken or vegetables and cook until partially done.
  5. Stir in yogurt, coriander, and mint leaves.
  6. In a separate pot, boil the soaked rice until 70% cooked.
  7. Layer the rice over the cooked masala mixture.
  8. Sprinkle saffron or coloring on top and cover tightly.
  9. Cook on low heat (dum) for 15–20 minutes.
  10. Fluff gently and serve hot.
